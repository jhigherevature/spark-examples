Most Common Pets

This article will give information about the most common pets in the US

Hopefully this information will give you a better idea about the different types of pets you can own.

Dogs

Dogs have a long association with people and have been selectively bred over millennia. They come in many breeds and can perform many roles.

Cats

Cats also have a long history with people and there are about 60 cat breeds in existence. Cats are just as loyal and affectionate as dogs, but they demonstrate their love in different ways.

Fish

Fish are popular because they simply require a large tank - but they do not need to go on walks and are relatively easier to care for than dogs and cats.
